My Works

Each project is my way of telling a story, expressing an idea, or capturing a moment. By sharing these works, I hope to connect with people who share the same interest in creativity and visual storytelling. My goal is not only to display my art but also to inspire others, show the passion behind my work, and make my content meaningful to those who see it.

Graphic Design Posters

My graphic design posters aren't the highest quality or the best in the room. However, they represent my abilities and skill level in terms of design and layouts. I always make sure that the posters I create are aligned to its purpose and its theme.

Digital Illustration

Here, i have my digital illustrations, which is my main interest and skill. I started my digital art journey since 2015, and overtime I have developed my own artsyle. My artsyle is semi-realistic with very stylized eyes and backgrounds. These artworks are my pride and joy. Even if AI comes to replace me, I will not stop creating my art because AI will never be able to copy the passion and love I pour into my creations.

Photography

Photography is not my forte, however I've poured my heart and soul into developing this particular skill. It is an all-around skill that brings joy to me as well as the people around me. I hope to be more confident in my photography skills in the near future, knowing the amount of effort I've exerted to improve.

Traditional Art

Traditional Art is something I've done since I was little, however I only took it seriously during the pandemic. That was when I developed various Traditional Art skills like colored pencils and graphite. As time passed, i eventually started using alcohol markers and acrylic paints as well, seeing how these mediums are most popular and efficient. I am extremely proud of this particular skillset because it takes so much patience but reaps great rewards.

3D Modeling

3D Modeling is a skill I never thought I could learn, but have always admired. Thanks to my professors patience and creativity, I was able to learn it quite well! I have learned both industry standard programs Maya and Blender thanks to her!

Animations

I started animation the moment I found out about digital art, however it didn't last long for me since circumstances have cornered me to move onto different things. Despite that, I was able to rekindle my interest during college and have produced a few shorts with the intent of continuing to pursue this passion. I hope to become an animator in the future, to be someone who will continue to strive for real human art for the people to see and find themselves in.
